Project Reporting

Monthly project reporting meeting sheet — Contract, Hours, Procurement and the PnL comparing Business Approval (at contract) with the Current Forecast. The PM fills the highlighted cells once a month and saves it, building the monthly history. Contract sales price and budgeted production & engineering hours come from Sales when the project is realized.
