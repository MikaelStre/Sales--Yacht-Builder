BHD B-models (engineering)

  • Step by step 
  • Create Detail Design Model (B-Model) for core routing, save to DDM
  • Load X-Model & UNLINK
  • Cut with H-block for straight edge, smaller surface for template
  • Offset outer contour -3mm
  • Suppress or delete the Hull / Deck trims
  • Load structural 2D drawing – to double check geometry and possible belt recessing
  • Open the penetrations to take actual sleeves as specified by Structural Engineers.

Example:
B121-01 Penetration sleeves
T1 = 6mm Cutout

T2 = 4mm Cutout

T3 = 5mm Cutout

      • capping in openings to be subtracted with 15% extra offset (Capping thickness +15%).
      • Check with production extra offset for penetration sleeves.
